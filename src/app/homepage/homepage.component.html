Slow Cooked

All our liquorices are slow-cooked to perfection. Each cooking process takes approximately 8 hours, reducing the original liquorice mass to about one-third. This gives the liquorice an extra soft and intense flavor.

A BITE OF NATURE

All our products are handcrafted in Bornholm. We are proud to say that we collaborate with a long list of Bornholm-based companies. These collaborations aim to use as many local ingredients as possible. For example, the oil used in the liquorices is produced on Bornholm from Bornholm ingredients.

DNA OF BORNHOLM

All our liquorices are produced with a focus on sustainability and natural flavors. For example, our berry flavors are made with freeze-dried berries, and our espresso flavor is made with freshly roasted coffee beans from a local roastery.
